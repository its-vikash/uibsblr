Parent/Student FAQs

How can I apply for my child’s admission?

You can apply online through our official website or visit the admissions office on campus. Our counselors will guide you through the process step-by-step.

What documents are required for admission?

You’ll need your child’s previous academic records, birth certificate, ID proof, and passport-sized photographs. Additional documents may be requested depending on the program.

What is the fee structure for various courses?

The fee varies by program. A detailed fee structure is available on our website or can be obtained from the admissions office.

Are there any scholarships or installment options available?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships and flexible installment plans to support deserving students.

Is transportation available for students?

Yes, we provide safe and reliable transportation across major city routes. Route maps and bus schedules are shared during admission.

What measures are taken to ensure student safety?

Our campus is secured with 24/7 CCTV surveillance, verified staff, and ID-based entry. We also conduct regular safety drills.

Are there medical facilities on campus?

Yes, we have a dedicated health room with a certified nurse and tie-ups with nearby hospitals for emergencies.
